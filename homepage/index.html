Good to know

Frequently asked

What exactly is 1MoreRep?
It's a multi-user gym tracker that runs in your browser and installs as an app. You log workouts, follow or auto-generate routines, track progress and body metrics, and compete in friendly weekly leagues. It's hosted for you today — and you'll be able to self-host it soon.
How much does it cost?
It's free to use — no subscriptions, no per-seat fees, and your data is never sold or monetised. Once self-hosting lands, you'll just need a server to run it on.
Do I need to be technical to use it?
Not at all — just open app.1morerep.de and sign in. Running your own instance will be an option soon, once we open-source on GitHub, for anyone comfortable with a Docker container.
Does the AI send my data anywhere?
No. The workout generator is deterministic by default. The optional AI assist runs against a self-hosted model — nothing is sent to a third-party AI cloud.
Will it work on my phone?
Yes — it's a PWA. Open it in your mobile browser and choose "Add to Home Screen" for a full-screen, app-like experience with opt-in push reminders. It works great offline, too.
Where do I sign in?
The app lives at app.1morerep.de. Use the sign-in page if you already have an account, or create one if registration is open on this instance.
